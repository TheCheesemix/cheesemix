Use your Cheese Bag

Keep your cheese tasting fresher longer with our Formaticum cheese bags. Manufactured in France, these porus bags are specifically designed to store cheese, let it breathe while retaining ample humidity to prevent the cheese from drying out.

Cheese and temperature

Keeping your cheese between 8-12 degrees is the optimum temperature for your cheese. We advise keeping your cheese on the bottom shelf of your fridge also so does not to chilled.
