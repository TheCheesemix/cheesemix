cheesemix is a new brand of dairy subscription products.

Founded to bring you quality dairy products and recipe ideas monthly. And to support independent dairy farms.

Packaged Moments

The cheesemix idea came from many moments with great friends. Whilst spent at one of our founders family farms in Devon. The cheese is packaged in a box we call the cheesecube. Its 100% recyclable and reusable to its core. And has modern insulation made from wool.

Quality Cheese

We work directly with our suppliers. Who cut and handpick each cheese or dairy product we select for each of our boxes. The suppliers produce is tested regularly. And we visit each one personally.

30 Minute Recipes

Your cheesecube includes a cheese recipe booklet. To enable you to use your cheese to its fullest. Cheese is not just for a cheeseboard. And can be used so many times. It is an amazing ingredient to use and makes brillant dishes.

Value & Convenience

It is hard to get to a deli every month. Though we all love the quality of a farm shop. It benefits the farmers after-all. We benefit the farmer, bring you the quality of the deli to your home or office. And give you cheese and dairy products when they are at their best.
