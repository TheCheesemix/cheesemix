Developing a diverse range of dairy subscription products

Co-Founder and Head of Marketing at Cheesemix - Amy Marks

Cheesemix's journey started with country side adventures with friends and family. Our company develops products that bring the best of the dairy industry to you. Our long term vision is to create innovative dairy products that challenge the industry.

For friends + Family

We wanted to develop a brand that was built around bringing family and friends together.

Our inspiration was taken from one of our founders family farms in Devon. Where over the years its become somewhat of the norm to entertain and introduce friends to the delights of local artisan produce from around the area.

This has created many memorable moments. And we wish to help create similar experiences for all our customers. Through the creation of subscription dairy products for your essential daily needs, snack food to dinner parties or cooking.

Supporting Dairies

From the outset upon our founders deciding to develop cheesemix. We wanted to meet with our suppliers as often as possible and get to know them personally.

It has always been important to us that they receive a fair price for their quality produce or services for creating our own label brands of dairy products.

So all the products that cheesemix launches will be fair prices for the manufacture, supplier or dairy farm. We also aim to lead the way in help pave the way for innovative production methods and dairy products.

Ethical + Environmental

We only choose producers and suppliers that look after their livestock to a high standard of living conditions and animal lifestyle.

It is also important that our producers maintain a high standard for handling food. From early production to consignment. This includes our fulfilment process for picking and packing our produce we supply.
