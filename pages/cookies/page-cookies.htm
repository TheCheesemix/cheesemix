Cookie Policy

Our frequently asked questions are designed to answer any questions you may have. If you can not find an answer here. Do please speak to use in real-time now by clicking me or email us on help@cheesemix.com.

Q. I’m interested in signing up for a Cheesecube? What are your fees?

The cheesecube is inclusive of delivery. For both subscriptions and one-off purchases. We do not charge you more than the stated price.

Q. I and pre-registered to cheesemix. What should I do now?

You should have been given a referral link in your welcome email or on the referral page you were redirected to upon signing up. Share a personal message or a cheesy moment via Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat, Twitter or Email. Making sure to include your referral link encouraging your friends to sign-up. You earn rewards for doing so. Read more in the referral program section of our FAQs.

Q. I just signed up! What should I do now?

Firstly thank you for signing up you are star. Well you should fill out your profile and answer any questions related to your interests in dairy products and cheese. To help us better understand your preferences. Then you should use your referral link you received when signing up and earn rewards by inviting your friends.

Do my cheeses really come direct from cheese maker?

Yes. We work with the cheese makers directly. They cut and wrap our cheeses. They then send them to our fulfilment provider. Who then packs and ships your cheesecube.

Q. Will you be launching an iOS or Android App?

Yes. We plan to launch an iOS and Android over the next twelve months. Once we have established a market fit.

Q. What does the cheese recipe booklet include inside?

The cheese recipe booklet includes insights into each cheeses in your cheesecubes origin and history, a story on the founders of the cheese makers or dairy or farm, an accompanying cheese recipe for each cheese in your box.

Q. Can I customise my cheesecube?

Currently no. Though we do look to add easily customisable cheesecube’s over the next twelve months. Suggest your cheesecube ideas here.

Q. Will you be offering the cheesecube in the United States or other European Countries?

Yes. We look to expand to the United States and other European Countries where we so the most demand for your product as soon as possible. You can sign-up to our newsletter to stay in informed here..

Q. Are you planning on offering weekly or bi-weekly subscriptions?

Yes. As we slowly add new product ranges to the cheesemix portfolio we definitely consider offering this.

Q. Can I pause my subscription?

Yes. All subscribers can pause their subscription at anytime. So if you go on holiday you can go to your subscriptions page here. And then select your subscription plan and select pause. You will not be charged during the period in which your subscription is paused.

Q. I’m not sure I want to continue my subscription. Can I cancel?

Yes. You can cancel at anytime by going to your account profile and then clicking subscriptions. Where you can then select and manage your subscription. If you are on a monthly subscription your subscription will end before the next payment is due. If you are a quarterly or annual subscriber your subscription your subscription will end on your next subscription renewal date.

Q. Can I purchase a Cheesecube without subscribing?

Yes. You can purchase a cheesecube either as a one-time purchase or through subscription.

Q. Is a one-time purchase of a cheesecube possible?

Yes. We do not just cater to subscriptions. And our cheesecube does allow you to purhcase one-time purchases for yourself or as a gift. Though we highly recommend subscribing to one of our regional cheesecubes to discover all our cheesecubes.

Q. What is the cheesemix Referral Program and how do I get involved?

It is easy to get involved. You simply need to pre-register currently or when our store launches very soon sign-up to cheesemix. After you have signed up or pre-registered you will be given and sent a referral link to share with friends. Enabling you to earn rewards.

Q. What does your referral program offer me?

Our referral program enables you to be rewarded for the more people you invite who sign-up to cheesemix.

Q. What payments do you take?

We take all major mastercards and debit cards. Including Bitcoin. The payment gateways we use are Stripe, Braintree, Paypal and Bitpay.

Q. Can I pay for subscriptions with Bitcoin?

At this time we only accept bitcoin for one-time purchases. In the future we intend to accept Bitcoin and other digital currencies for all our subscriptions.

Q. How do your promotional codes work?

You may only apply one promotional code to each order. And if you have used a promotional code within an order before you will not be able to use it again.

Q. When do I receive my cheesecube?

You will receive your cheesecube on the last Thursday or Friday of the end of month.

Q. Do you deliver cheesecubes outside of the United Kingdom?

Currently we only deliver cheesecubes in the United Kingdom. Excluding the Isle of Man, Scotland, Northern Ireland and Guernsey.

Q. Will my cheesecube fit in my mailbox?

No. Your cheesecube is 180x180x180mm. We have tried to keep the cheesebox as small as possible. Though due to the cheeses being various sizes it was hard to make a box any small. Would you like snack sized cheese boxes? Let us know here.

Q. Can I return a cheesecube?

Unfortunately no. The cheesecube once its has been delivered is non-returnable. Between shipping and dispatch our cheese and cheesecube is owned by cheesemix. Though once you receive the product you are the sole owner and we are not liable for your cheesecube.

Q. My item was damaged during transportation? What do I do?

If your cheesecube has been damaged during transportation. Our box is rugged enough that it should protect your cheese that is snuggly kept inside by its cheeseblanket (Woolcool insulation). Though obviously the unexpected happens. So please contact us on help@cheesemix.com. And we will do the best we can to resolve the situation. Otherwise our standard policy is that although we own the cheesecube and its content during transportation it is not our liability. And is a liability of the courier services under their terms of service and or insurance policy.
