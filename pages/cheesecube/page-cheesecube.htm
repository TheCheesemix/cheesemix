Meet the cheesecube

Our super cheesecube is not actually drawn to scale. Though its representation is as a snug and wonderful as shown below.

Your cheesecube arrives in a 100% recyclable and reusable premium box. The environment and our customers experience is our number one priority.

Upon opening your cheesecube your unique monthly cheese recipe booklet presents itself to you. This includes: cheese maker stories, recipes and the origin and history of each cheese.

Your artisan cheeses are wrapped at the cheese maker. And come in our unique cheese pouches or cheese storage bags to keep your cheese fresh for longer.

Your cheesecube includes five cheeses between 200 - 250g each. Enough for two weeks of recipes or a cheese board for 8 - 10 people. The booklet is 24 pages long.
